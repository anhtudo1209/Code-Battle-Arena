GAMEPLAY Why does my code execute differently in the arena?
The Arena environment runs on a strict 500ms timeout per turn. If your algorithm uses excessive recursion or heavy computation, it may be terminated prematurely by the Arbiter System to maintain game pacing.
GAMEPLAY How is ELO calculated after a draw?
ELO adjustments are based on expected outcome probability. If both players have similar ratings, the change will be minimal.
