# Basic Arithmetic of 2 Numbers

Print the sum, difference, product, and quotient of two numbers entered from the keyboard. The results should be rounded to 3 decimal places.

## Input
Two real numbers `a` and `b`

## Output
Four values: sum, difference, product, and quotient (`a/b`) of the two numbers `a` and `b`, each value on a separate line

## Sample Input
2 3

## Sample Output
5.000
-1.000
6.000
0.667
