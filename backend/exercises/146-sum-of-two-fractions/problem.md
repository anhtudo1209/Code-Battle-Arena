# Sum of Two Fractions

Given two fractions a/b and c/d, where a, b, c, d are integers.  
Find the simplified form of the sum a/b + c/d.

## Input
Line 1: two integers a and b  
Line 2: two integers c and d

## Output
The simplified fraction of the sum in the form numerator/denominator

## Sample Input
1 2
3 4

## Sample Output
5/4
