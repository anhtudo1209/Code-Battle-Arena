# Number of Terms

Enter a positive integer `N`. Then answer the following two questions:

**Question 1:** How many positive integers are less than or equal to `N` and greater than or equal to `(N-1)/2`?  
**Question 2:** How many positive integers are greater than or equal to `N + 1` and less than or equal to `N^2`?

## Input
A single positive integer `N`

## Output
- Line 1: Answer to Question 1  
- Line 2: Answer to Question 2

## Sample Input
5

## Sample Output
4
20
