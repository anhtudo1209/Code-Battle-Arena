# The Shoes

One day, Cristiano Ronaldo wanted to count how many pairs of shoes he currently has. After checking, he found that he has n red shoes and m blue shoes.

Ronaldo is a fashionable player who always creates unique styles on the field. For each match, he wears one red shoe on his left foot and one blue shoe on his right foot. After the match, he takes off the shoes and gives them to his fans.

Your task is to help Ronaldo determine how many matches he can play following this style. After he can no longer wear one red and one blue shoe, he will switch to wearing two shoes of the same color. Determine how many additional matches he can play in this case.

## Input
A single line containing two integers n and m (1 ≤ n, m ≤ 10^9), representing the number of red shoes and the number of blue shoes.

## Output
Two integers:
1. The number of matches Ronaldo can play wearing one shoe of each color.
2. The number of matches he can play afterward wearing two shoes of the same color.

## Sample Input
7 3

## Sample Output
3 2
