# Difference of Two Fractions

Given two fractions a/b and c/d, where a, b, c, d are integers.  
Find the simplified form of the difference a/b - c/d.  
If the result is negative, the minus sign should appear in the numerator.

## Input
Line 1: two integers a and b  
Line 2: two integers c and d

## Output
The simplified fraction of the difference in the form numerator/denominator

## Sample Input
1 2
3 4

## Sample Output
-1/4
