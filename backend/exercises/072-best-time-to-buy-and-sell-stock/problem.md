# Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock

You are given an array prices where prices[i] is the price of a given stock on day i. Find the maximum profit you can achieve by choosing a single day to buy and a single day in the future to sell. If no profit is possible, return 0.

## Input
An array

## Output
Find the maximum profit you can achieve by choosing a single day to buy and a single day in the future to sell.

## Sample Input
6
7 1 5 3 6 4

## Sample Output
5
