# School Rules

According to the school regulations, each case of not wearing a student ID card will deduct `a` merit points from the class, each case of talking in class will deduct `b` merit points, and each case of being late will deduct `c` merit points. The attendance book records that in the month there are `t` cases of not wearing ID cards, `n` cases of talking in class, and `m` cases of being late.  

Enter the values `a`, `b`, `c`, `t`, `n`, `m` from the keyboard and calculate the total merit points deducted in that month.

## Input
Line 1: The values `a`, `b`, `c`, `t`, `n`, `m`
Line 2: The amount of cases of broken rules.
 all values are seperated by a space
## Output
The total merit points deducted in the month

## Sample Input
1 1 1 1 1 1
1 2 3 4 5 6
## Sample Output
21
