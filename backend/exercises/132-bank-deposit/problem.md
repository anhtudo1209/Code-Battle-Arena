# Bank Deposit

Mr. Bo has an initial sum of N VNĐ. He plans to deposit this money into a bank with an annual interest rate of 10%. At the end of each year, he will withdraw the interest and add it to the principal amount to deposit for the next year (compounding annually). He intends to withdraw the entire amount once his total savings reach M VNĐ to buy a cow. The question is: After how many years will Mr. Bo have enough money to buy the cow?

## Input
Two integers N and M (10 <= N <= M <= 10^9).

## Output
The number of years required to save the money.

## Sample Input
100 160

## Sample Output
5
